French Bulldog

French Bulldogs can't swim, due to their short nose, short legs and weight.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Weight: 12Kg
  • Avarage Lifespan: 11-13 years
  • Origin: France

The French Bulldog is a breed of domestic dog, bred to be companion dogs. They are stocky, compact dogs with a friendly, mild-mannered temperament. They are great for appartments!
